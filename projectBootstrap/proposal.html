Introduction:

We, the Unknown Group, are a small company that dedicates its resources in order to provide web platforms that can assist in social issues with dynamic information.

Team:

We consist of a team of sophomores in the Software engineering department of the Institute of High Studies of Technology of Monterrey (ITESM). We have covered the basics of computer programming, the basic concepts of object oriented programming and we are currently cursing the web design course. Members: Diego Bernardo Sanchez Micalco, Fernando Martinez, Marcelo Estrada Medina.

Problem:

The problem we try to solve as a company. We as a company have the mission of inform to the people about the problems that affect our society in a deep or superficial way.

Strategic Goal:

Our goal is to produce high quality web projects that more than just being “pretty” to be really interactive and functional to the purpose of its creation, like inform, impact, or entertain, in order to solidify our existence in the market and later expand our range of expertise in other technology based projects.

Methodology:

  1. Extensive talk with the client in order to understand the purpose of the project.
  2. Recommend a template to the client based on the talk, however the client can select another alternative.
  3. Let the client sketch a high concept of the page.
  4. Starting adapt the selected template to a prototype based on our understanding of the client motif.
  5. Show the client the prototype in order to verify the direction, if the prototype is approved we go on, tweak it if necessary.
  6. based on the approved prototype start the development process.
  7. Upload the page online
  8. Give maintenance and support to the page for one year.

Implementation Plan:

The plan depends mostly in the motif behind every project, but generally it its based on the methodology as the starting point, later adapt our base methodology into every situation/challenges that presents towards the development process.

Cost:

We charge our services based on the time invested on your project, and the complexity of the project itself. As a client you will select what type of page are you looking for, but however if you want a custom page out of the templates, and you want us to create a whole new structure, it will cost more in a considerate way.

TYPE PRICE
Simple Project 50$
Medium Project 80$
Large Project 120$

Considerations:

We give free support to the page for one year after the online realese, however if you want maintance to the web page after that year you will be charge depending of the project's complexity.
