The four 'W' plus one 'H':
  1. Who: Everyone who needs to use the main streets to move from one part of the city to another by car or the public bus.
  2. When: Everyday when you're moving from one place to another you're always haunted by badly made streets, badly patched streets, or flat out broken ones. Specially in Summertime during rain season.
  3. Where: in the metropolitan zone of Guadalajara.
  1. Why: To cut costs on making and fixing streets and because the government doesn't bother to fix it most of the time.
  2. How: It affects people that want to go anywhere in the street because it damages cars, makes rides unpleasant, and sometimes it makes it so you can't go to certain places.
