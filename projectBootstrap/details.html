What

  • The problem we are trying to to get known is the huge amount of streets broken or in bad conditions that affect many cars by causing damage to the cars that go in this streets.

Who

  • Everyone who is authorized to drive, have the need to use the any street from the city of Guadalajara, including the main strees to move from one part of the city to another by car or the public bus.

When

  • Everyday when you're moving from one place to another you're always haunted by badly made streets, badly patched streets, or flat out broken ones. Specially in Summertime during rain season.

Where

  • In the state of Jalisco, the area delimited by the metropolitan zone of the city of Guadalajara, which includes: Zapopan, Guadalajara, Tonalá, Tlaquepaque, El Salto, and Tlajomulco de Zuñiga.

Why

  • To cut costs on making and fixing streets and because the government doesn't bother to fix it most of the time.

How

  • It affects people that want to go anywhere in the street because it damages cars, makes rides unpleasant, and sometimes it makes it so you can't go to certain places.
